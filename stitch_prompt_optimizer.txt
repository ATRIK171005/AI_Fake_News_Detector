# DESIGN SYSTEM & AESTHETICS (STRICT REQUIREMENTS):
- Framework: Pure HTML5 with Tailwind CSS (using Tailwind CDN with custom extended colors & container queries).
- Dark Mode Default: Use a deep obsidian maritime navy background (#0a0e17) with slate glassmorphic surface panels (rgba(22, 30, 46, 0.85)) and subtle glowing cyan borders (border-primary/30).
- Color Palette (Maersk Cyber-Industrial Theme):
  * Primary Accent (Maersk Electric Ice Blue): #1e88e5 / #40a9ff (used for active routes, primary buttons, and vessel icons).
  * Secondary Accent (Optimization Green): #00e676 (used for CO2 reductions, fuel savings, and optimized status badges).
  * Warning/Delay Accent (Maritime Amber): #ffab00 (used for port congestion, weather warnings, and route deviations).
  * Surface Containers: #121824 and #1a2232 with subtle backdrop-blur-xl.
- Typography: Import 'Plus Jakarta Sans' for ultra-clean geometric body text and 'JetBrains Mono' for all logistics coordinates, waybill IDs, ETAs, and financial/carbon metrics. Use bold industrial hierarchy with tight letter spacing (-0.01em) for headings.
